San Francisco Real Estate Market Overview

San Francisco embodies the West, where people go to shed the old and become the new. Over the centuries, its Spanish colonists, Gold Rush opportunists, hippies - and now, tech entrepreneurs - have built futures in the City by the Bay that would impact the rest of the world. These groups and others have molded the city's provocative values, culinary excellence, stunning architecture, ethnic diversity, and constan...t artistic and technological innovation. Set at the northern tip of the San Francisco peninsula, SF is lush with verdant hills and the natural beauty typical of Northern California. Come enjoy the views. Or just come with an idea. Insider Tips: Where to Go

Morning

Enjoy two of the city's favorites: fancy coffee and South American dance. Grab a cup of Philz single-brew coffee and let it fuel your drumming class at ABADA.

Afternoon

The best way to soak in the view: walk up the Lyon Street Steps, then follow the SF Heritage mansion tour. Bet on which is the senators and which belonged to Levi Strauss.

Evening

You can't live in San Francisco without going to a drag show, and you can't live without dinner. Kill two birds with one stone at Asia SF, the Cal-Asian restaurant notorious for its drag queen dinner shows.
