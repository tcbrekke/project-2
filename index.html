In Project 2, we explore the change in housing prices in the Bay Area. The change in housing prices will be visualized on choropleth and heat maps. We will compare the change in housing prices to the inflation rate, the stock market, and local minimum wage laws. The story we hope to find in the data is the relationship between the relative strength of the economy, housing prices in certain geographic regions, and the relationship between booms and busts.
